About NameverseHub

NameverseHub is the most comprehensive baby name database on the web, covering 105,966 unique names with meanings, origins, and real popularity data sourced from the US Social Security Administration.

Our Data

Every name on NameverseHub includes actual US Social Security records from 1880 to 2025 — not estimates or guesses. We analyze over 375 million records to bring you accurate popularity trends, peak years, and total usage counts.

Our Mission

We believe every name has a story. Our mission is to document and preserve the history behind every name, making it easy for parents, researchers, and the curious to discover the meaning, origin, and cultural significance of any name.
